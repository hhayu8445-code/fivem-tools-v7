╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                    TROUBLESHOOTING                           ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

❌ Problem: Cannot add A record
✅ Solution: Delete old A record first, then add new one

❌ Problem: CNAME conflict
✅ Solution: Delete old CNAME for www, then add new one

❌ Problem: Domain still shows old site
✅ Solution: 
   - Clear browser cache
   - Wait 30 minutes for DNS propagation
   - Test in incognito mode

❌ Problem: SSL Certificate error
✅ Solution:
   - Wait 10 minutes after DNS propagation
   - Vercel auto-generates SSL
   - If still error, contact Vercel support
